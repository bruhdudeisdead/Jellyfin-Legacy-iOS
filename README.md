# Jellyfin for Legacy iOS
A Jellyfin client for legacy iOS devices. That's pretty much it. You can listen to music and watch TV shows or movies.

To use, enter your instance's URL (e.g. `http://192.168.0.123:8096`) in the Settings app, and log into your profile using your username & password or Quick Connect.

**If you try to play video in MKV format, it will not load (the player will show up and then go away immediately) because MKV is not supported by iOS.**
